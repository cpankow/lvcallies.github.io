The LVC Allies are a safe and confidential resource on dealing with harassment and bullying within the LVC. Our goal is to listen, foster an inclusive and harassment-free environment, and provide you with a safe space to discuss your options and how you want to proceed. We are a judgement-free zone.

We are available at all times via email at lvcallies@gmail.com.
